## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome new maintainers! The path to becoming a maintainer typically involves:

1.  **Consistent Contributions**: A track record of high-quality pull requests (features, bug fixes, or documentation) over a sustained period (usually several months).
2.  **Community Engagement**: Active participation in issue triage, code reviews for other contributors, and technical discussions.
3.  **Demonstrated Understanding**: A deep understanding of the project's architecture, goals, and coding standards.

If you are interested in becoming a maintainer, please reach out to the current maintainers to express your interest. New maintainers are appointed by the BDFL.
